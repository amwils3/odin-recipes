Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as regu, bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheeses
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Tomato
  • Lasagna
  • Mozzarella
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake
